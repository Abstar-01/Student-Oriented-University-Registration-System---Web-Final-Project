Welcome to the Student Course Registration Page!

This page is your one-stop hub for managing your academic course registration. Here, you can easily register for your semester courses based on your batch or add any courses you may have missed in previous terms. To make your registration smoother, the page also provides complete banking details for the university’s partner banks — so you can conveniently make payments through the bank that works best for you.
